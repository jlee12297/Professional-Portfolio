About Me

Hi, my name is Josh, and I am an aspiring full-stack web developer based in the Seattle area. I am currently learning the ins-and-outs of the coding world through a University of Washington Coding Bootcamp provided by Trilogy.

Outside of coding, I'm very passionate about fitness, and just pushing towards a healthy lifestyle in general. I take pride in coaching aspiring lifters who want to learn basic lifting form and progressions through programming, and overall contributing to making better environments in gyms for people from any background to work towards a healthy in a safe, judgement free area.

As a developer, I hope to be able to make the same impact on the web. Making a variety of resources and pages accessible, and taking on creative projects that could be useful to just the right person. I'll update this portfolio with all of my noteworthy projects for any and all who are interested!
